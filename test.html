About Multi-bouw

Since Moshe Grunhut started Multi-bouw 13 years ago, we've successfully completed over 200 construction projects, including highly complex renovations. We pride ourselves on combining skilled craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver high-quality, sustainable solutions for both private and commercial clients.

Our commitment to meticulous planning, clear communication, and impeccable quality ensures every project is completed to the highest standards, with safety and client satisfaction at its core.
